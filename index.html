LIFE

Saeeda: "I don't wanna get married, I'm only 10...."

Saeeda is a 12-year-old widow who lives in one of the villages of Razavi Khorasan, Iran.

According to Saeeda, in this village, if a girl finishes her primary education and remains unmarried, she will soon be stigmatized and will be blamed for her faults.

Saeeda is a child who got married at the age of 10 and separated from her 42-year-old husband a year and a half later.

Saeeda: "Because our financial situation was not good, I decided to go to my husband's house without a dowry..."

Saeeda: "My marriage loan also belonged to my father, and a week later, at the age of 10, I was forced to sit at the wedding table..."

Saeeda: "My husband said that I should either stay and endure or leave his life because I had no place in my father's house..."

Saeeda: "I had to continue living with a man who brings other women home under the pretext of not being able to do some chores..."
